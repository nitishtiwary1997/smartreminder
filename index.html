Privacy Policy – Smart Reminder

Effective Date: 03 January 2026

Smart Reminder respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use the Smart Reminder application.

1. Information We Collect

Smart Reminder does not collect any personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

The app only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Reminder titles and descriptions
  • Reminder date and time
  • Notification and ringtone preferences

This data is used solely to provide reminder functionality.

2. How We Use Information

The information stored in the app is used only to:

  • Create and manage reminders
  • Send notifications at scheduled times
  • Improve app performance and reliability

Smart Reminder does not send your data to any server.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All reminder data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload, sync, or share your data with any external services
  • Reasonable security measures are used to protect stored data

4. Permissions Used

Smart Reminder may request the following permissions:

  • Notification permission – to alert you at reminder times

These permissions are required only for core app functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Smart Reminder does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising SDKs.

6. Children’s Privacy

Smart Reminder does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the app is safe for general use.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
